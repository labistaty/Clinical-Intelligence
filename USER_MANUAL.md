# User Manual
## Clinical Intelligence Expert System

Welcome to the Clinical Intelligence Expert System. This guide explains how to interact with the system to receive diagnostic assistance.

### 1. Starting a Consultation
1. Navigate to the **Symptom Analyzer** page.
2. You will see a list of common symptoms (Fever, Cough, etc.).

### 2. Selecting Symptoms
1. Click on the tiles representing the symptoms you are experiencing.
2. Once a symptom is selected, you must provide additional details:
   - **Severity**: Use the slider to indicate how severe the symptom is (1 = Mild, 10 = Severe).
   - **Duration**: Use the +/- buttons to indicate how many days you have had the symptom.

### 3. Running the Analysis
1. After selecting all relevant symptoms, click the **Analyze Symptoms** button at the bottom of the screen.
2. The system will process your inputs through its inference engine.

### 4. Reviewing Results
1. The **Diagnosis Result** page will display:
   - **Likely Diagnosis**: The condition identified by the expert system.
   - **Confidence Score**: How certain the system is based on the rules.
   - **Reasoning**: A detailed explanation of why this diagnosis was chosen.
   - **Treatment Protocol**: Recommended steps for recovery.

### 5. Managing Records
1. You can save the diagnosis to a patient record by filling out the **Patient Details** form.
2. View past consultations in the **Patient History** section.
3. Monitor physiological trends in the **Vitals Dashboard**.
